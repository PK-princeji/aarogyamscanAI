Aarogyam ScanAI

AI-Based Medical Imaging Analysis System

🌟 Our Mission: Care with AI & Compassion

At Aarogyam ScanAI, we believe healthcare should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our mission is to bridge the diagnostic gap, especially in rural areas, by combining the warmth of "Aarogyam" (good health in Sanskrit) with the precision of AI. We are dedicated to making medical diagnostics smarter, faster, and more widely available.

Our system provides AI-powered healthcare support that assists patients and doctors alike, with reports available in multiple languages for clear, easy-to-understand insights.

🔹 What We Do: Instant Analysis, Actionable Reports

Fast Diagnosis: Get results in less than 5 seconds per scan.

High Accuracy: Our models achieve over 90% accuracy in detecting common diseases.

Visual Insights: We provide heatmaps to highlight affected regions.

User-Friendly Reports: Receive a downloadable, structured report with diagnostic suggestions.

🩺 Our Solutions: Diseases We Detect

🫁 Lung Diseases

Upload your chest X-ray to detect Pneumonia, Tuberculosis (TB), Pneumothorax, Cardiomegaly, and early signs of lung cancer.

🧠 Brain Conditions

Upload your brain CT or MRI scan for detection of Brain Hemorrhage, Stroke, Brain Atrophy, and Tumors.

🦴 Bone & Spine Issues

Upload your X-ray or MRI images to check for minor and major fractures, spinal cord injuries, slipped discs, and joint injuries.

🩺 General Health Reports

Upload your blood test reports or medical prescriptions for medicine recommendations, diet suggestions, and risk analysis.
