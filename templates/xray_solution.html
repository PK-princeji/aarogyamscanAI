Primary Pathologies & Clinical Focus

Our deep learning models have been fine-tuned to detect the top three respiratory anomalies responsible for the highest global mortality rates. Early detection of these specific vectors is critical.

Pneumonia Detection

Clinical Manifestation: Alveolar inflammation leading to fluid or pus accumulation, severely restricting oxygenation and causing acute respiratory distress.

Why We Chose It: Pneumonia is the apex infectious killer of infants globally. Our AI isolates bilateral consolidations instantly, allowing pediatricians to initiate life-saving antibiotic therapy without waiting for manual radiological reports.

Tuberculosis (TB) Screening

Clinical Manifestation: Granulomatous lesions, cavitation, and severe structural degradation of pulmonary tissue caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis.

Why We Chose It: The epidemiological burden of TB in developing nations is catastrophic. Manual screening bottlenecks lead to prolonged asymptomatic transmission. AarogyamScanAI breaks this chain by providing instant, localized screening camps with immediate results.

Lung Cancer Detection

Clinical Pathogenesis: Uncontrolled cellular proliferation within pulmonary tissues, often manifesting as malignant pulmonary nodules, masses, or pleural effusions.

Why We Chose It: Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer-related mortality globally. Early-stage micro-nodules are notoriously difficult for the human eye to detect on standard X-rays. Our CNN isolates these anomalies in their infancy, drastically improving patient survival rates through early intervention.

The Collapse of Traditional Diagnostics

AarogyamScanAI was engineered as a direct response to the systemic failures of the conventional medical imaging pipeline.

A. The Radiologist Deficit

The global healthcare infrastructure faces a catastrophic shortage of trained radiologists. In rural sectors, the radiologist-to-patient ratio can be as severe as 1:100,000. Patients endure grueling travel simply to have an X-ray film analyzed.

WHO Global Workforce Data: Highlights that specialized diagnostic personnel shortages directly inflate the mortality rates of treatable respiratory diseases by over 40% in emerging economies.

B. Cognitive Fatigue & Human Error

Radiologists are human. After analyzing hundreds of complex grayscale images over a 12-hour shift, visual fatigue degrades diagnostic accuracy, leading to false negatives (missed early-stage tumors or micro-lesions).

RSNA Clinical Studies: Retrospective audits indicate that human diagnostic error rates can peak at 30% under high-volume stress conditions, compromising patient safety.

C. Prohibitive Socio-Economic Costs

The cumulative cost of specialist fees, film printing, and logistics places an unbearable financial burden on marginalized communities, forcing many to abandon treatment entirely.

World Bank Health Report: Identifies out-of-pocket diagnostic expenses as a primary catalyst pushing vulnerable populations beneath the poverty line.

Deep Learning Architecture

How our neural engine processes raw medical imagery into clinical intelligence.

01

Image Pre-processing

Raw DICOM/JPEG files are normalized. Noise reduction algorithms and CLAHE (Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalization) enhance pulmonary structures.

02

Feature Extraction (CNN)

The image passes through multiple Convolutional and Pooling layers (e.g., ResNet/DenseNet architecture). The network extracts low-level edges to high-level pathological patterns.

03

Classification Layer

Fully Connected (Dense) layers compile the extracted features. Softmax/Sigmoid activation functions generate a probabilistic output for the presence of Pneumonia, TB, or Lung Cancer.

Ecological Sustainability vs. Medical Waste

Traditional radiology is an environmental hazard. AarogyamScanAI is a green technology initiative.

The Traditional Ecological Hazard

  • Chemical Toxicity: 'Developer' and 'Fixer' chemicals used in darkrooms are highly toxic. Improper disposal leaches heavy metals into groundwater ecosystems.
  • Silver Halide Contamination: Traditional films are coated with Silver Halide. When disposed of in landfills, these compounds degrade soil microbiomes permanently.
  • Carbon Emission Footprint: Manufacturing plastic polymer films and the physical transportation of reports generate a massive, unnecessary carbon footprint.

The AarogyamScanAI Resolution

  • 100% Digital & Chemical-Free: By shifting to cloud-based neural networks, we completely eradicate the need for toxic processing fluids and plastic films.
  • Zero Physical Waste: No paper envelopes, no plastic sheets. The entire lifecycle of the diagnosis is secured in sustainable data centers.
  • Decarbonizing Healthcare: Instant digital transmission means zero physical travel for report transfers, drastically reducing the overall carbon emissions of the healthcare sector.
